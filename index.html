About Null

The Hyderabad Chapter

null is the open security community for curious learners, working professionals, researchers, builders, and bug hunters. Null Hyderabad brings that spirit to the city through free, volunteer-driven, in-person community events.

The chapter is open to newcomers and experienced practitioners alike developers, defenders, offensive researchers, leaders, students, and anyone serious about learning security. Monthly meetups are usually held on the third Saturday, with deeper hands-on sessions scheduled throughout the year.

What We Do

Learning by doing.

Recurring formats that keep the community active, sharp, and connected throughout the year.

Monthly Meet

The regular community meetup for talks, news bytes, networking, and sharing what the local security scene is learning right now.

Every 3rd Saturday

Humla

A hands-on offensive security workshop focused on practicing real attack techniques in a guided, safe setting. Think red-team drills.

Offensive · Hands-on

Bachav

A hands-on defensive security workshop built around prevention, detection, hardening, and practical blue-team thinking.

Defensive · Hands-on

Community Discussion

Conversations continue between events through speaker proposals, social posts, mailing lists, and community-led follow-ups.

Ongoing · Async

All of this exists for one reason: to make the web a more secure place.
